# Summary

Chain guide to suit maslow CNC machine

Use original motor mount screws

Print one of each left and right and 2 pins.

The pins are inserted into the lower screw hole to lock the chain in place, remove to remove chain. 

Printed in PLA 0.25 layer height using large flat side as the base printing the mounting area as the Z axis. 

Fusion 360 files and STL files provided.
